Colin Estes

Director of Software Engineering, MRI/NASA

Collin Estes is the Director of Software Engineering for MRI Technologies in Houston TX. Collin has been leading the software engineering for NASA's EMU spacesuit contract at Johnson Space Center since 2008. In 2014, Collin and the MRI Dev Team started architecting and implementing NASA's first Node.js enterprise architecture for the agency's ExtraVehicular Activity (EVA) Office, platform still in use and expansion today. Beyond EVA, Collin works to promote open source and collaboration across NASA.
